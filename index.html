Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive. If you have more, feel free to ask!

What is the procedure for endoscopic surgery?

Endoscopic surgery involves minimal incisions and uses a camera to guide the surgeon. It's used for treating kidney stones and other conditions, offering faster recovery and less pain.

What types of laser surgeries are available?

We offer laser surgery for various conditions, including prostate and kidney stone diseases. Laser surgery is minimally invasive, leading to quicker recovery times and reduced risks.

Do you offer cancer surgery for kidney, prostate, and bladder?

Yes, our specialists provide advanced surgical treatments for kidney, prostate, and bladder cancer. We use the latest techniques to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

How are urological birth defects in neonates and children treated?

Our team of specialists offers comprehensive treatment for urological birth defects in infants and children, ensuring the best care for their long-term health.

What treatments are available for urinary diseases in women and senior ladies?

We provide specialized treatments for urinary tract infections and other urinary diseases in women and senior ladies. Our approach includes both medical and lifestyle management to improve quality of life.

How do you handle infertility and incontinence issues?

We offer advanced evaluation and management for infertility and incontinence. Our specialists use the latest treatments to help manage these conditions and improve the well-being of our patients.
